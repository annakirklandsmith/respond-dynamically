Accessibility Statement

OC Connect Mobile is committed to providing a website that is accessible to everyone. To further this commitment, we’re working to implement Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.0, which provides standards for making web content more accessible for all site visitors, including people who are blind or visually impaired.
